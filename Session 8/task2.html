FIFA World Cup 2014 News

The biggest surprise in the history of the FIFA World Cup qualifiers — and indeed in the history of international football — was recorded on 15 April 2001, when Australia beat American Samoa 31–0.
This legendary match also brought global acclaim for Archie Thompson, whose 13-goal haul set a new world record, which stands to this day, for an individual player in a single international match.
And though the defeat crushed American Samoa's openness, so inspiring has their subsequent recovery been that it is now the subject of an acclaimed documentary, 'Next Goal Wins', showing across the world.
